# Fabled Flow — Releases

Official releases of **Fabled Flow**, the 100% on-device dictation app
for macOS. Audio never leaves your Mac.

**[Download the latest version](https://github.com/Lume-Apps/fabled-flow-releases/releases/latest)**

After downloading: open the DMG and drag Fabled Flow into Applications.
The app is not yet notarized — on first launch, right-click the app and
choose **Open**.

`latest.json` in this repository is the update feed the app checks —
at launch, once a day, and from Settings → General → Check for updates.
The check downloads only this file and sends nothing about you or your
Mac.
